# FL-GreenGuard v1.0.0 - Release Notes

## 🚀 Official Launch: The Masterpiece is Live

**FL-GreenGuard** is now available on Google Play Store. Designed by a Florida Licensed Professional (#LF301625) for 100% FDACS compliance.

---

## ✨ What's New in v1.0.0

### 🧮 Smart N-Calculator
- **Accurate 0.5 lb/1000 sq ft nitrogen math** based on your fertilizer bag analysis
- Real-time Green/Red compliance status
- Supports both quick-release and slow-release nitrogen percentages
- Prevents over-application with automatic warnings

### 📍 Ring of Responsibility
- **GPS-enabled alerts** to keep you 10 feet from water bodies
- Real-time buffer zone monitoring during applications
- Haptic feedback when approaching protected areas
- Offline map caching for areas without cell signal

### 📋 Audit-Proof Logs
- **Automatically capture** wind speed, temperature, and GPS coordinates for every application
- Offline-first logging — works even without internet
- Cloud sync to Supabase when you're back at the shop
- Export logs as PDF for inspection documentation

### 💼 Digital Wallet
- **Store your GI-BMP Certificate and LF License** for instant proof during inspections
- Upload PDFs or take photos directly from the app
- Expiration date tracking with renewal reminders
- Full-screen "Show for Inspection" mode — no need for physical documents

### 📊 Dashboard & Analytics
- Quick stats: Applications this week, compliance rate, pending syncs
- One-tap navigation to all features
- GPS lock status indicator
- Weather integration (wind, temperature)

### ⭐ Pro Subscription ($4.99/month)
- Unlimited audit log storage
- Advanced analytics and compliance reports
- Priority support
- "Restore Purchase" button for new devices
