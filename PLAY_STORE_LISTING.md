## Full Description

### Professional Landscaping Compliance Made Easy

FL-GreenGuard is the official compliance tool for Florida's 70,000+ licensed landscape professionals. Built by a licensed pro (LF301625) to meet 100% FDACS requirements.

#### Smart N-Calculator
Stop guessing. Get accurate nitrogen application rates in seconds. The app calculates 0.5 lb/1000 sq ft limits automatically based on your fertilizer bag analysis. Real-time Green/Red status keeps you compliant.

#### Ring of Responsibility
GPS monitoring keeps you 10 feet from water bodies. Real-time alerts notify you when you're approaching protected areas. Haptic feedback ensures you never miss a warning. Works offline too.

#### Audit-Proof Logs
Every application is automatically logged with timestamp, GPS coordinates, wind speed, and temperature. Cloud sync to Supabase when you're back at the shop. Export as PDF for inspections.

#### Digital Wallet
Store your GI-BMP Certificate and LF License in the app. No more fumbling for documents during inspections. Full-screen "Show for Inspection" mode. Expiration reminders keep you current.

#### Pro Subscription ($4.99/month)
Unlock unlimited audit logs, advanced analytics, and priority support. "Restore Purchase" button for new devices. Cancel anytime.
